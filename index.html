Welcome to the State of Michigan’s GI Bill® approved facility search engine. This search engine allows you to search for GI Bill® approved colleges/universities, non-degree schools (certificate granting), Department of Labor approved apprenticeships, on-the-job training programs, vocational flight schools, and approved license and certification test/exams.

On this page, you can search for Approved Facilities and view the Approved Programs for each facility.

If you'd like to search by approved programs instead, use the Approved Program Search link in the navigation MENU in the top right corner of this page and select program search.

GI Bill® is a registered trademark of the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA). More information about education benefits offered by VA is available at the official U.S. government website at http://www.benefits.va.gov/gibill

Help

You can search for Facilities by Facility Name, Facility Type, and Facilities within a certain radius from a particular address/city or a combination there of.

Facilities matching your search criteria will be listed and also pinned on the map.

Selecting a Facility will display the programs approved for veteran educational benefits offered by the selected Facility.

If your school, facility or program is not listed please feel free to have your employer or a school representative contact our office at misaa@michigan.gov
